General Terms: - Graph: Diagram, chart - Axis: X-axis, Y-axis - Data point: Wert, Datenpunkt - Trend: Trend, Entwicklung - Fluctuation: Schwankung - Peak: Höchstwert - Trough: Tiefpunkt - Increase: Anstieg - Decrease: Abnahme - Stability: Stabilität - Range: Spanne, Bereich Describing Changes: - Rise: Anstieg (e.g., "The sales rose by 10%.") - Fall: Rückgang (e.g., "There was a sharp fall in revenue.") - Grow: Wachsen (e.g., "The market grew steadily.") - Decline: Rückgang (e.g., "A gradual decline was observed.") - Remain stable: Stabil bleiben (e.g., "The figures remained stable over the year.") - Soar: In die Höhe schießen (e.g., "Profits soared in Q3.") - Plummet: Stark abfallen (e.g., "The stock prices plummeted after the news.") - Level off: Sich einpendeln (e.g., "The growth leveled off after June.") Adjectives and Adverbs for Trends: - Sharp: Scharf (e.g., "a sharp increase") - Gradual: Allmählich (e.g., "a gradual rise") - Steady: Stetig (e.g., "a steady growth") - Dramatic: Dramatisch (e.g., "a dramatic drop") - Slight: Leicht (e.g., "a slight improvement") - Significant: Signifikant, bedeutsam (e.g., "a significant change") Statistics-Specific Terms: - Average: Durchschnitt - Median: Median - Mean: Arithmetischer Mittelwert - Mode: Modus (häufigster Wert) - Standard deviation: Standardabweichung - Variance: Varianz - Percentage: Prozentsatz - Correlation: Korrelation - Distribution: Verteilung - Outlier: Ausreißer Types of Graphs: - Line graph: Liniendiagramm - Bar chart: Balkendiagramm - Pie chart: Kreisdiagramm - Histogram: Histogramm - Scatter plot: Streudiagramm - Area chart: Flächendiagramm Interpretation Phrases: - The graph shows...: Der Graph zeigt... - According to the chart...: Laut der Grafik... - There is a clear trend of...: Es gibt einen klaren Trend von... - As illustrated in the graph...: Wie in der Grafik dargestellt... - This indicates that...: Dies zeigt, dass... - The data suggest that...: Die Daten legen nahe, dass...
